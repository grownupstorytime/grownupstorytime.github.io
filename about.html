ABOUT

What is Grown-up Storytime (GUST)?
Houston's monthly live storytelling event.

How does it work?
You send us stories, we vet and assign them to a crack-squad of expert storytellers. Since 2007 we've been lucky to collaborate with hundreds of talented writers, performers, hosts, producers, venues, and well-wishers. Together we entertain thousands.

How can I help?
Come to a show! Once you get a feel for GUST, we always need writers, readers, hosts, and guest producers.

When and where?
3rd Tuesday of every month. Doors 7p, show 8p. Continental Club at 3714 Main Street. Or stream virtually. $10 cash-only at the door. Or pre-pay online. Dining, cocktails, paid garage, and free street parking nearby. Check Insta for the latest.
