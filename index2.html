how

You send us stories. We vet and assign them to a rotating crack-squad of expert storytellers. Stories and performers are crowdsourced, workshopped, and prepped beforehand by our team (currently Blair and Emily). Stories should be personal, engaging, funny, sad, that thing you just had to tell someone, etc.

when

Doors at 7p. Show at 8p every third Tuesday at Continental Club. 3714 Main Street. Ten dollars cash-only day-of-show. Ten dollars online before day-of-show. Dining, free street parking and paid garage nearby. Follow on Insta for the latest.
